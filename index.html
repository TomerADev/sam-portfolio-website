About Me

Sam Shaw is a photographer from the NJ/NYC area. She currently attends The College of New Jersey majoring in Visual Arts with a specialization in Lens Based Art and is proficient in Photoshop and Adobe Premiere. Her photos aim to explore the simple beauty that life has to offer and bring the uniqueness out of the mundane. She also features different sources of light and how they interact with the space.
